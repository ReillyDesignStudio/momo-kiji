# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the momo-kiji community will experience harassment-free participation, regardless of age, body size, caste, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or any other dimension of diversity.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or email address, without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Community members who violate this code of conduct may be temporarily or permanently removed from the community at the discretion of maintainers.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, or have any other concerns, please contact the project maintainers:

📧 **robert.reilly@reillydesignstudio.com**

All reports will be reviewed and investigated. We are committed to maintaining confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.
